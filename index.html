MBTI: Which Game of Thrones character are you?

Find out which Game of Thrones character you are in just 5 minutes!

Explanation

This 5-minutes quiz is based on the Myers Briggs Type Indicator test, a very popular personality test many organizations uses to determine a person's personality. This may be useful when it comes to checking if a candidate is a right fit to a company's cultural values, or whether you're more likely to value justice or forgiveness.

Instructions

When responding to the statements, of the two responses please choose the one you agree with most. If you are not sure how to answer, make your choice based on your most typical response or feeling in the given situation. To get a reliable result, please respond to all questions. When you are done with answering, press the “Score It!” button at the bottom of the screen.
